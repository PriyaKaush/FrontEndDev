A tribute to "sulphur men"

Somewhere in Indonesia...

Looking at him, He might weigh around 60 kilos, but he was carrying 75 kilos of sulphur ore on his shoulder! Two baskets balanced on a beam on his shoulders

We started our trek at midnight into the volcanic craters, when we drove through the chilled forest routes, there was no one to be seen. As we approached the crater, we started spotting these men carrying hand made baskets, two of them connected over shoulder pole.

As we started our trek with all preparations like helmet with light, N95 Masks, Thick gloves and warm clothing, we saw these men with worn out clothes and thongs going down the dangerous craters without any protective equipment
